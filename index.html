I'm a trilingual Communications Specialist with over a decade turning dense scientific and policy content into compelling narratives for diverse audiences. My work spans EU-funded research consortia, institutional journalism, podcast production, and data-informed content strategy.

From coordinating Horizon Europe dissemination for a biotechnology project at the University of Coimbra, to building media relations for a public research institute in Brazil — I bring a newsroom-like approach to every brief: accuracy, speed, and storytelling instinct.

Currently based in Madrid, I work at the intersection of science communication, strategic messaging, and content creation for international and European contexts.
